WHO ARE WE?

At Smith & Associates, our legacy is built upon two decades of dedicated service to the people of Connecticut. Specializing in personal injury law, we have successfully represented clients in a myriad of cases, from workers compensation claims to slip and fall accidents, and everything in between. With a deep understanding of the intricacies of the legal system, our team of experienced attorneys is committed to advocating tirelessly for the rights of our clients, ensuring they receive the justice and compensation they deserve.

Founded on principles of integrity, compassion, and excellence, Smith & Associates is more than just a law firm; we are trusted allies and advocates for those facing the challenges of personal injury. Our longstanding presence in the community has allowed us to cultivate strong relationships with clients, earning their trust through transparent communication and unwavering dedication to their cases. With a focus on personalized service and a track record of successful outcomes, we stand ready to navigate the complexities of the legal process on behalf of our valued clients.
